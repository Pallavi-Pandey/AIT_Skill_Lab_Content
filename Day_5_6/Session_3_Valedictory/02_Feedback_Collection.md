# Feedback Collection

> **Purpose:** Your feedback directly shapes the next version of this bootcamp. Please be honest, specific, and constructive — the same standard we asked you to apply to your capstone insights.

---

## Why Your Feedback Matters

This bootcamp is a living program. The content, pacing, lab difficulty, tool choices, and session structure are all revised after every cohort based on student feedback.

When you fill out this form:
- Sessions that were unclear get rewritten with better examples.
- Labs that were too easy or too hard get recalibrated.
- Topics that students want more of get expanded in the next version.
- Facilitator delivery that did not land gets coaching.

Your feedback is not anonymous criticism — it is a contribution to the students who come after you.

**Please answer every question. For rating questions, circle or tick your rating. For open-ended questions, write at least one complete sentence.**
