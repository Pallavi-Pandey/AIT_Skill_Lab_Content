# Day 5 & 6: Capstone Completion & Valedictory

> **The Final Lap.** Everything you have practised over the past four days — Python, Pandas, SQL, Visualization, and BI Dashboards — converges here. You will complete your capstone project, present it to a jury, and celebrate your achievement.

---

## Overview

Day 5 & 6 is divided into three focused sessions that take you from a work-in-progress capstone all the way to a polished presentation and a well-deserved certificate.

| # | Session | What Happens |
|---|---------|--------------|
| 1 | **Capstone Development** | Finalize your dashboard, validate insights, and prepare documentation ready for submission. |
| 2 | **Project Presentation & Evaluation** | Present to a jury panel in a 10-minute slot, receive structured scores, and participate in a feedback debrief. |
| 3 | **Valedictory Ceremony** | Reflect on the full program journey, collect feedback, discover your next learning steps, and receive your certificate. |

---

## Sessions

### Session 1: Capstone Development
The dedicated heads-down work session. Your team will apply the finishing touches to your dashboard, stress-test every insight against the raw data, and produce the documentation package that goes alongside your visual deliverable.

- [01 - Dashboard Finalization](Session_1_Capstone_Development/01_Dashboard_Finalization.md)
- [02 - Insight Validation](Session_1_Capstone_Development/02_Insight_Validation.md)
- [03 - Documentation Preparation](Session_1_Capstone_Development/03_Documentation_Preparation.md)
- [Lab - Capstone Final Review](Session_1_Capstone_Development/Lab_Capstone_Final_Review.md)

### Session 2: Project Presentation & Evaluation
Each team delivers a structured 10-minute business presentation to a jury panel. Evaluations are scored against a published rubric covering dashboard quality, insight depth, clarity, teamwork, and Q&A handling. A structured feedback debrief follows all presentations.

- [01 - Group Presentation Guide](Session_2_Presentation_Evaluation/01_Group_Presentation_Guide.md)
- [02 - Jury Evaluation Rubric](Session_2_Presentation_Evaluation/02_Jury_Evaluation_Rubric.md)
- [03 - Feedback Session](Session_2_Presentation_Evaluation/03_Feedback_Session.md)

---

### Session 3: Valedictory Ceremony
The closing session of the bootcamp. Students reflect on the full five-day journey, complete a program feedback form, explore recommended next steps in data analytics, and receive their certificates of completion.

- [01 - Program Reflection](Session_3_Valedictory/01_Program_Reflection.md)
- [02 - Feedback Collection](Session_3_Valedictory/02_Feedback_Collection.md)
- [03 - What's Next](Session_3_Valedictory/03_Whats_Next.md)

---

## Learning Outcomes Achieved

By the end of Day 5 & 6, students will have demonstrated the ability to:

- Build and present a production-quality interactive BI dashboard
- Validate analytical insights against raw data with documented reasoning
- Communicate data-driven decisions clearly to a non-technical audience
- Give and receive structured, constructive peer feedback
- Chart an independent learning roadmap beyond the bootcamp
